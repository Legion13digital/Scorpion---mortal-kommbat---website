HISTORY

Scorpion, also know as Hanzo Hasashi, is a resurrected ninja and one the most iconic characters in the Mortal Kombat franchise. Once of the Shirai Ryu cian, killed by Sub-Zero of the rival Lin Kuei cian.

Resurrected by the sorcerer Quan Chi, Scorpion became a spectre with hellfire powers, driven by revenge for the death of his family and cian. His signature weapons are a kunai attached to a rope, which he uses to spear opponents and them toward him, famously yelling "Get over here!".

Throughout the Mortal Kombat series, Scorpion has walked the line between anti-hero and villain, often serving his own agenda while occasionally aiding the forces of good when their interests align.

ABILITIES

Hellfire

Scorpion can control and summon hellfire, using it to enchance his attacks or teleport through flames.

Kunai Spear

His signature weapon - a kunai attached to a rope that he uses to impale opponents and pull them toward him.

Teleportation

Scorpion can teleport short distances, often appearing behind his opponents to deliver davastating attacks.

Martial Arts Mastery

As a former Shirai Ryu grandmaster, Scorpion is highly skilled in various forms of martial arts and weapon combat.

Undead Physiology

As a spectre, Scorpion cannot be killed by conventional means and possesses supernatural strength and durability./p>

Demon form

Scorpion can transform into a fiery demon, significantly increasing his power and hellfire abilities.
